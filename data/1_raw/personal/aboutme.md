# About Me

Hi, I’m Alex. I’m a software engineer with 10 of experience building reliable, user-focused solutions. For the past four years, I worked at Clinithink, where I collaborated with an incredible team until the company unfortunately closed. While it was tough to see colleagues and friends go through that experience, it also gave me time to reflect on what I value most in my work: teamwork, creativity, and building technology that makes people’s lives easier.

## A Journey of Growth

Recently, I’ve been pushing myself through side projects including this portfolio. After a final-stage rejection from a role I was excited about, I received some fantastic feedback that highlighted both my strengths and growth areas. I’m grateful to the people who shared that feedback with me; it’s helped me sharpen my skills and inspired me to explore new ideas like using TDD and AI-assisted development for animations on this site.

## Life Beyond the Code

Outside of work, I’m a parent of two, which has taught me more about patience, problem-solving, and resilience than I could have ever learned in a classroom. Balancing family and career gives me a strong sense of purpose and a drive to build a “new normal” that’s both stable and meaningful.

## Looking Forward

I’m excited about the opportunity to bring my skills and the lessons I’ve learned along the way to a new role where I can grow, contribute, and create impact alongside a great team.
